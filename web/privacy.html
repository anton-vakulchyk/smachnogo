Privacy Policy

Effective: June 11, 2026 · smachnogo (“the app”), operated by Anton Vakulchyk

smachnogo helps you track what you eat. We collect the minimum needed to do that, and nothing for advertising.

What we collect

  • Food photos you scan. Compressed copies are uploaded to our servers (Amazon Web Services, US) for analysis. Location metadata (GPS) is stripped from photos on your device before upload.
  • Meal descriptions you type for AI estimation.
  • Your food diary — meals, estimated calories and nutrition, dates.
  • An anonymous account identifier. The app creates a random account on first use. No name, email, or phone number is collected or required.
  • Purchase status (when subscriptions launch) via Apple — we never see your payment details.

AI processing

To estimate calories and nutrition, your food photos and meal descriptions are sent to Google's Gemini API for processing. They are processed to produce the estimate and are subject to Google's API terms. We do not use your photos or descriptions to train models.

Retention

  • Uploaded photos: deleted from our servers within 90 days (a thumbnail stays on your device only).
  • Scan processing records: deleted within 30 days.
  • Your diary: kept until you delete it or your account.

Deletion & export

Settings → Export my data gives you your full diary as JSON. Settings → Delete account & data permanently removes your diary, photos, and account from our servers, immediately. Deleting your account does not cancel an active subscription — manage subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.

What we don't do

  • No advertising, no ad trackers, no analytics SDKs that profile you.
  • No selling or sharing of your data with anyone beyond the processors above (AWS hosting, Google AI processing, Apple payments).
  • No health data is read from or written to Apple Health.

Important note about estimates

Calorie, macro, and nutrition figures are AI estimates intended for general tracking. They are not medical or dietary advice.
