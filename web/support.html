Common questions

A scan looks wrong

Estimates are AI-generated and improve with clear, well-lit photos taken from above. You can adjust the portion (¼–2×) or any details after saving — tap the meal in your diary. For shakes and mixed dishes, answer the follow-up question for a much better estimate.

A scan failed or is stuck

Failed scans don't count against your limits. Your photo stays on the device — tap the entry in the diary to retry. Scans also resume automatically when you're back online.

How do I move a meal to another day or mark a planned meal as eaten?

Tap the meal → change the day or toggle “Planned”.

How do I export or delete my data?

Settings (gear icon) → Export my data or Delete account & data. Deletion is immediate and permanent. An active subscription is managed separately in your Apple ID settings.
